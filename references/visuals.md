# Visual Neuromarketing

The image decides before the copy is read — the brain processes visuals ~60,000x faster than text. Use this when advising on ad creatives, post images, thumbnails, or landing page visuals.

## Faces

- A human face is the strongest attention magnet (fusiform face area fires involuntarily)
- **Gaze direction steers the viewer:** a face looking AT the viewer grabs attention; a face looking at the headline/CTA transfers attention to it. Use looking-at-viewer for stopping the scroll, looking-at-CTA for conversion pages
- Authentic, imperfect faces outperform stock-photo perfection (authenticity premium — see ai-consumer-journey.md)

## Contrast & attention

- The feed is the context: whatever the platform's dominant look is (bright, polished, AI-generated), do the opposite (Von Restorff)
- One focal point per image — the brain gives up on cluttered visuals in ~0.3s (cognitive fluency)
- Text on image: max 6–8 words, high contrast. The image hook mirrors the post hook, it doesn't repeat it

## Color psychology (directional, not absolute — context and culture dominate)

- Red/orange: urgency, appetite, energy — good for CTAs and deadlines
- Blue: trust, calm — finance, health, B2B
- Green: money, growth, "go"
- Black/gold: premium, exclusivity
- The rule that actually matters: **the CTA color must contrast with everything around it** (isolation effect beats any specific color)

## Numbers & price display

- Show prices in smaller font than the benefit (large price font = larger perceived pain)
- Strike-through anchor price next to the real price, always
- Odd, specific numbers in image copy ("৩৭% বেশি sale") read as data; round numbers read as claims

## Before/after & demonstration

- The brain trusts what it sees over what it reads — show the transformation, product-in-use, or real screenshot instead of describing it
- Before/after side-by-side is loss aversion + endowment in one image

## Checklist for any creative

- [ ] One focal point, readable in 0.3s
- [ ] Face present? Gaze pointed where attention should go
- [ ] Different from the surrounding feed, not just "nice"
- [ ] Image hook ≤ 8 words, complements (not repeats) the caption hook
- [ ] CTA/price displayed with contrast + anchor
