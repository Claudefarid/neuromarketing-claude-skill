# 🧠 Neuromarketing — Claude Skill

Apply brain-based buying psychology to your marketing copy, automatically.

**~95% of purchase decisions happen subconsciously.** This skill teaches Claude how your customer's brain actually decides — then writes ad copy, landing pages, offers, and pricing that speak to it.

Created by [Fariduzzaman Swadhin](https://linkedin.com/in/fswadhin) — AI Specialist & Product Growth Manager.

## What it does

- **Diagnoses your buyer** — cold/warm/hot intent, real objections, what they fear losing
- **Selects the right triggers** — 20 proven principles (loss aversion, social proof, anchoring, scarcity, decoy pricing…) matched to your buyer's situation
- **Writes brain-compatible copy** — ads, landing pages, pricing pages, offers, email/DM, social posts
- **Audits existing copy** — tells you exactly what's killing conversion and rewrites it
- **Explains every trigger it uses** — you learn the psychology, not just receive text

## Install

### Claude Code
```bash
mkdir -p ~/.claude/skills/neuromarketing
git clone https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/neuromarketing-claude-skill.git ~/.claude/skills/neuromarketing
```

### Claude Desktop / Claude.ai
Upload `SKILL.md` (and the `references/` files) to a Project, or paste the contents as project instructions.

## Use

Just ask naturally:

- *"Audit my landing page copy — why isn't it converting?"*
- *"Write ad copy for my course targeting freelancers"*
- *"Design a 3-tier pricing page for my SaaS"*
- *"Make this post more persuasive"*

Claude will diagnose the buyer, pick 2–4 triggers, write the copy, and list the psychology behind every choice.

## Ethics

This skill amplifies truth — it refuses fake scarcity, fake testimonials, and invented statistics. Fake triggers convert once and destroy your brand permanently.
